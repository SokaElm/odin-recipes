Carrot Cake Recipe

Description

This is the best carrot cake recipe I ever tested. It comes from the famous French pastry chef Philippe Conticcini

Ingredients

  • 15 g of unsalted butter
  • 20 g of black treacle
  • 3 egg whites
  • 4 egg yolks
  • 290 g of light brown sugar
  • 4 g of cinnamon
  • 3 g of mixed spice
  • 1 g of salt
  • 70 g of chopped walnuts
  • 4 g of bicarbonate of soda
  • 230 g of peanut oil
  • 270 g of strong white bread flour
  • 30 g of cornflour
  • 11 g of baking powder
  • 400 g of grated carrots
  • 2 vanilla pods
  • 1 unwaxed orange peel

Steps

  1. Make the batter: Beat the wet ingredients together, then mix in the dry ingredients. Stir in the carrots and fold in the pecans.
  2. Bake the cake: Pour the batter into the prepared cake pan or pans. Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick comes out clean.
